Navicon Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

Summary

Navicon does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. All customization data is stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.

Data Navicon Stores

  • Your tiles, folders, sections, and layout settings
  • Your theme and UI preferences

This data is stored locally on your device and is not sent anywhere.

Data Navicon Does Not Collect

  • Browsing history
  • Web activity
  • Personally identifiable information
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information

Network Access

Navicon does not make network requests for analytics, tracking, advertising, or profiling.

Third Parties

Navicon does not share data with third parties.

Contact

If you have questions, contact: support@navicon.app
